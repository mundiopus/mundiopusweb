Privacy Policy

✦

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mundi Opus ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data submitted through the newsletter subscription form on mundiopus.com, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What data we collect

When you subscribe to the Mundi Opus newsletter, we collect only your email address and a record that you gave consent to receive emails.

Why we collect it

Your email address is collected solely to send you occasional updates from Mundi Opus: new video announcements, behind-the-scenes content, and channel news. We will never use it for any other purpose.

Legal basis (GDPR Article 6)

We process your data based on your freely given, informed, and explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you provide by ticking the checkbox and submitting the form.

How your data is stored

Submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party form service that acts as a data processor on our behalf. Formspree stores your email securely and does not sell or share it with any third parties. You can read Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Who has access to your data

Only the owner of the Mundi Opus channel has access to your email address through the Formspree dashboard. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing or any other purpose.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, your data will be removed promptly.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request that we delete your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe at any time with no consequences
  • Lodge a complaint — with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD) at aepd.es

How to unsubscribe or delete your data

To unsubscribe or request deletion of your data, send an email to mundiopuscontact@gmail.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at mundiopuscontact@gmail.com.
